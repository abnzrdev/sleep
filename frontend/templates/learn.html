Learn

Practical sleep concepts that make the data easier to understand.

LunaSleep AI is built around interpretable sleep signals. These short guides explain the concepts behind the score, the monitor, and the AI coach.

Sleep Efficiency

What is sleep efficiency?

Sleep efficiency is the share of time in bed that you are actually asleep. Higher efficiency usually means less time spent awake after falling asleep and a more consolidated night.

Awakenings

Why awakenings matter

Frequent awakenings can fragment sleep, reduce restorative stages, and lower sleep efficiency even when total time in bed looks adequate.

Caffeine

How caffeine affects sleep

Caffeine can delay sleep onset, lighten sleep, and increase the chance of overnight disruption, especially when taken later in the day or in larger amounts.

Movement

How movement tracking helps

Movement patterns can provide useful signals about restlessness, awakenings, and posture shifts. They do not tell the full story alone, but they add valuable context.

AI Guidance

How AI can explain sleep data

AI can summarize trends, answer questions in plain language, and connect the score with habits like caffeine timing, bedtime consistency, or repeated awakenings.
