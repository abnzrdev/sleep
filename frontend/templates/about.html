About LunaSleep AI

A sleep-focused product built around explanation, not just measurement.

LunaSleep AI brings together machine learning, natural-language guidance, and connected movement sensing to help users understand sleep efficiency in context.

ML Prediction

Structured sleep scoring

The predictor estimates sleep efficiency from age, duration, stages, awakenings, caffeine, and lifestyle context.

IoT Monitoring

Live movement context

The monitor keeps live sensor telemetry visible so motion, posture, and awakenings can be reviewed alongside predictions.

AI + NLP

Plain-language coaching

The AI coach explains scores, answers sleep questions, and helps translate data into realistic behavior changes.

Product Direction

What LunaSleep AI is for

The product is designed for people who want a calmer way to understand sleep quality: a guided score, a live movement view, and an AI coach that can explain what may matter next.

What it does not do

LunaSleep AI does not replace clinical evaluation or diagnose medical conditions. It is built to support interpretation, reflection, and healthier nightly habits.
